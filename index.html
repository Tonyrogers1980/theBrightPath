FAQ

Common questions

Is BrightPath aligned to the UK curriculum?
Yes. Every lesson is generated specifically for the UK National Curriculum at the correct key stage for your child's age — KS1 (ages 5–7), KS2 (7–11), KS3 (11–14), and KS4 (14–16). Topics, vocabulary, and question style all match what your child's school is teaching.
Does it work for children with dyslexia or learning difficulties?
Yes — this is something we designed deliberately. Every lesson has a built-in read-aloud button that reads the full lesson text aloud at adjustable speeds (slower, normal, faster). Children with dyslexia, ADHD, or any reading difficulty can listen to the lesson rather than reading it, and then focus on answering the questions at their own pace.
How is it different from other learning apps?
Most apps use pre-written question banks. BrightPath generates a completely fresh lesson every time — new content, new questions, new examples — tailored to your child's exact key stage. It also marks written answers with specific feedback on each one, not just multiple choice ticks. And the weekly parent report tells you exactly what Professor Hoot said about each lesson.
How long does a lesson take?
Each lesson takes 20–30 minutes. The content reads in around 10 minutes (or listen with read-aloud) and answering the questions takes another 10–15 minutes. There are 2 lessons per subject per day. Most families do 2–3 subjects on 2–3 days per week.
Can my child redo a lesson to improve their score?
Yes — every marked lesson has a Redo button. Previous answers are pre-filled so your child only needs to edit the parts that needed improvement. They can see Professor Hoot's feedback on each question and use it to write a better answer. This is one of the most effective ways to actually learn from mistakes rather than just moving on.
Is my child's data safe?
Yes. We collect only the minimum data needed — your email and your child's first name, age, and lesson responses. We never sell data, never show advertisements, and comply fully with UK GDPR. All data is secured so your child's information is only ever visible from your parent account.
What devices does it work on?
BrightPath works on any device with a modern browser — iPhone, iPad, Android tablet, laptop, Chromebook, or desktop. No app download required. It is fully optimised for touch screens so tablets work great.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel at any time from the Parent settings page inside the app — no phone calls, no emails, no forms. You keep full access until the end of your billing period. No surprise charges.
