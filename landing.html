FAQ

Common questions

Is BrightPath aligned to the UK curriculum?
Yes. Every lesson is generated specifically for the UK National Curriculum key stage matching your child's age — KS1 (ages 5–7), KS2 (7–11), KS3 (11–14), and KS4 (14–16).
How is my child's privacy protected?
We collect only the minimum data needed — your email, your child's first name, age, and their lesson responses. We never sell data, never show ads, and comply fully with UK GDPR. Children's data is only accessible to their parent account.
Can I use BrightPath on a tablet?
Yes — BrightPath works on any device with a modern browser: iPhone, iPad, Android tablet, laptop, or desktop. No app download required.
How long does a lesson take?
Each lesson takes about 20–30 minutes. The content reads in around 10 minutes, and answering the questions takes another 10–15. There are 2 lessons per subject per day — one introductory, one deeper.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel at any time from the Parent settings page inside the app. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
Can my child redo a lesson?
Yes — every marked lesson has a "Redo" button. Previous answers are pre-filled so your child can read the feedback and improve specific answers rather than starting from scratch.
